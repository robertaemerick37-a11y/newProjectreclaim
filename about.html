About

Who We Are

GetReclaim is a support and guidance platform dedicated to assisting individuals who have been affected by online scams and financial fraud. We work with victims of investment scams, cryptocurrency fraud, romance scams, impersonation schemes, and other forms of online deception.

Our purpose is to help individuals understand what has happened and identify the type of scam involved. We provide guidance on filing complaints, organizing relevant information, and recognizing common scam tactics, helping victims take informed and responsible next steps.

Online scams continue to evolve, using increasingly sophisticated methods to target both individuals and businesses. At GetReclaim, we stay informed about emerging fraud patterns and use this knowledge to support victims with clear, structured information and practical direction.

We believe no one should feel alone or confused after being scammed. Our mission is to provide clarity, awareness, and professional support—empowering individuals to move forward with confidence and better protect themselves against future fraud.

Support When You Need It Most

WE UNDERSTAND

Experiencing a scam can leave you feeling overwhelmed, frustrated, and unsure of where to turn. Many victims are left without clear answers after the scammer disappears and financial institutions are unable to reverse the transaction. We understand how isolating this situation can feel.

TAKE THE NEXT STEP

Taking action early is important after a scam has occurred. By documenting what happened and filing a complaint, you create a clearer path forward. Our role is to guide you through this process and help you understand the options available to you.
